DBMS PROJECT ON LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

DESIGN OF REPEATED SONG PLAYLIST USING CIRCULAR LINKED LIST

This project introduces a novel approach to designing a repeated song playlist utilizing a circular linked list data structure. The playlist is conceptualized as a series of interconnected nodes, each representing an individual song and storing relevant metadata such as song name and artist. The circular linked list is employed to establish a continuous loop, enabling repetitive playback seamlessly. The structure ensures that the final node in the playlist is linked back to the initial node, forming a closed loop for perpetual playback.Playback functionality is achieved through traversing the linked list, initiating from any point within the playlist and progressing through the sequence of songs. This circular arrangement facilitates an infinite loop, facilitating the seamless and automatic repetition of the playlist.
